Privacy Policy

Last updated: 29 June 2026

This policy explains what AYILON Arena collects, why, and how we handle it. By using the service you agree to this policy.

1. What we collect

  • Account info: email address, nickname, country, and a securely hashed password.
  • Exchange data (read-only): the API key you connect, your exchange UID, and the account equity, profit/loss, and trading volume we read to compute rankings. These keys are read-only — they cannot trade, transfer, or withdraw.
  • Technical: a session token stored in your browser to keep you logged in.

2. How we use it

  • To run the competition: compute scores, build leaderboards, and award prizes.
  • To verify your email and secure your account (verification codes, optional 2FA).
  • To verify, where applicable, that your exchange account is registered under AYILON's referral for prize eligibility.

3. What is shown publicly

Leaderboards and the Hall of Fame display your nickname, country, and competition stats (rank, return, profit, volume). Your email, API keys, and account identifiers are never shown publicly.

4. Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We use third-party providers only to operate the service (e.g. infrastructure hosting and email delivery for verification messages), and exchange APIs to read your competition stats.

5. Security & retention

Passwords are hashed. API keys are read-only and used solely to read your stats. We retain your data while your account is active. You can disconnect your exchange key at any time to stop all data access, and you can delete your account to remove your data.

6. Your choices

  • Disconnect your API key — stops all reading immediately and removes you from active leaderboards.
  • Change your nickname at any time.
  • Request account deletion via the contact below.

7. Contact

Privacy questions or deletion requests: support@ayilon.com
