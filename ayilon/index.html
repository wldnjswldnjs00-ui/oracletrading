Our Approach

Institutional-grade logic. Now yours.

The same logic that works in a bull market works in a bear market. AYILON identifies institutional S/R levels and enters in the direction the market is already moving.

BTC/USDT
ENTRY LOGIC
Multi-Touch S/R Detection
Levels are only validated after 3+ confirmed price reactions. The algorithm measures wick precision and body rejection — not just proximity.
Wick-Only Entry Filter
Entry triggers only on 5m wick contact with the level — never on a body breakout. The same technique used by institutional desks.
Volume Pattern Confirmation
Volume spikes at the level confirm institutional participation. Low-volume touches are skipped — only high-conviction setups are taken.
Ranging Market Filter
Detects choppy, low-structure phases and pauses trading automatically. Prevents repeated stop-losses during consolidation.
The algorithm that learned not to lose first.
Every position is monitored in real time. Volatility-based position sizing, dynamic stop-loss adjustment, and circuit breakers that automatically pause trading during extreme market conditions — because protecting your capital always comes before making profit.
